About Orange Medical Plan

Tired of juggling with your health care? At Orange Medical Plan we offer you, as our member, a unique advantage in health care delivery and quality. We strive to keep prices affordable with our competitive array of plans. Orange Medical Plan is quickly becoming the new industry medical plan leader!

If you are currently without a healthcare plan, recently lost your insurance, a 1099 employee, in the "gap", and need a medical plan. Apply for your orange medical card today! The orange medical plan can also be a valuable addition to your current medical plan with our outstanding medical, dental vision, and pharmacy options.

Orange Medical Plan has a professional panel of executive team members who excel at handling medical situations and are devoted to finding solutions for your specific needs. With all the problems we all face dealing with todays economy, we are prepared to provide you affordable options without sacrificing the quality of care.

Mission

Our mission is to offer you, our member, access to the highest quality, most affordable, efficient, complete and cost-effective alternative to health care insurance.
